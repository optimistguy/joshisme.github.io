Websites that help you relaxing and be more productive at learning.

Sometime you find your self not productive in learning stuff, other time you can not relaxing? You are in the right place, to day I introduce you to some websites that help you in relaxing and much more, make you more productive in learning!

  1. brain.fm
    Brain.fm is a really great website to make you productive in learning. Just sign up! Login and play musics that brain.fm provied. There are ‘Focus’, ‘Relax’ and ‘Sleep’ musics that you can play, that are really effective on what they intented to do! There are a pro version that will give you more feautures like offline access, but for me the free version if nice enough! So that is brain.fm
  1. Pocket
    Pocket is a ‘watch later’ button for your browser and your time. If you see an interesting website but you need to do something first. Just press the ‘Poket’ button and Pocket will save that website to your lists. After done looking at the website, remove it!. It help you save time searching in your browser’s history!
    Make you do what you need to done first, so that is Pocket!
  1. Evernote
    As you may know, Evernote is on of the most popular talking note thingy. But not so many of you know that Evernote have a web-app. Evernote is not just a note-talking website. It is also a life saver. For me it is a productivity suite! It save me lots of hours. And if you use my link, you will have 3 months pro! So that is Evernote.

Thanks for reading everyone!
